My goal for this project was to analyze and compare the offensive and defensive performance of the Los Angeles Dodgers from 2020-2023.For the offense I analyzed home runs,OPS(how frequently a batter can reach base and hit for power), and batting average. For defense,I analyze strikeouts,ERA(Earned Runs Average),and WHIP(Walks + Hits Per Innings Pitched)
